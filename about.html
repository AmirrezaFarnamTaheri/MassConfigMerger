A Free & Open Internet for All.

ConfigStream is an open-source project dedicated to providing free, reliable, and auto-updating VPN configurations to help you bypass censorship and browse freely.

Our Mission

We believe that everyone has the right to access information without restriction. Our mission is to empower users worldwide with the tools they need to stay connected and informed, bypassing digital barriers and promoting a more open internet.

How It Works

Our system, powered by GitHub Actions, works tirelessly to scan public sources for new proxies. Each one is rigorously tested for speed, security, and reliability. The best ones are automatically compiled into fresh configuration files, updated every three hours.

An Important Notice on Privacy

These configurations use free, public proxies. While we diligently filter for malicious nodes, we cannot guarantee the privacy of your data. We strongly advise against using these proxies for sensitive activities. For casual browsing and bypassing geo-restrictions, they are a powerful free tool. Use at your own risk.
